# DESIGN SYSTEM: RITUAL (Streetwear Aesthetic)

## Brand Vision & Motto
**Motto:** "Fashion for the wild at heart."
**Vision:** A premium, cinematic e-commerce experience blending high-fashion minimalism with bold, architectural streetwear elements.

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | OKLCH | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Terracotta** | `#C85D4D` | `oklch(55% 0.16 25)` | Hero background, accents, primary brand color. |
| **Onyx Black** | `#000000` | `oklch(0% 0 0)` | Primary text, large headings, buttons, dark sections. |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | `oklch(100% 0 0)` | Backgrounds, high-contrast text. |
| **Muted Gray** | `#E5E5E5` | `oklch(91% 0 0)` | Product card backgrounds, borders, muted elements. |

## Typography

### Headings
- **Font:** **Syne** (Extra Bold / Black)
- **Style:** All caps for impact. Architectural, wide, and geometric.
- **Tracking:** Tight for large display headers (e.g., "RITUAL"), wide for section headers (e.g., "LATEST ARRIVALS").

### Body
- **Font:** **Inter** or **Geist**
- **Style:** Clean, high readability.
- **Letter Spacing:** Slightly increased for a luxury feel (0.05em).

## UI Components & Design Language

- **Border Radius:** `0px` (Strictly rectangular). No rounded corners to maintain an architectural, sharp aesthetic.
- **Spacing:** High whitespace. Large "sections" with ample breathing room (128px+ between major blocks).
- **Buttons:**
  - Solid Black background, White text.
  - No radius.
  - Hover: Background transition to Terracotta or slight opacity change.
- **Product Cards:**
  - Full-frame images on light gray backgrounds.
  - Typography centered or left-aligned below the image.
  - Uppercase product titles and prices.

## Layout Patterns

1. **Hero Split:** Large typography overlaid on high-contrast color blocks.
2. **Marquee:** Horizontal scrolling text (e.g., "RITUAL RITUAL RITUAL") in thick, outline or solid styles.
3. **Grid Layout:** 4-column product grids with no visible borders, utilizing background contrast for separation.
